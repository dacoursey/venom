What are the details?

While we don’t know everything yet, we have some of the basic info that you’ll need.

Who?

Boys and girls grades 6-12. Competitions are broken up using SC weight class guidelines..

What?

Folk style wrestling, with a splash of freestyle.

When?

We run outside of the school wrestling season. Practice is Monday - Thursday 6-8 PM.

Where?

Catawba Ridge High School wrestling room. Park in the gym parking lot and walk down the hill path to the metal door marked with a red number 22.

Why?

If you’re new to the sport of wrestling, it’s unlike any other sport. Your child will learn to overcome hardship, gain confidence and strength, and build bonds that last a lifetime.

Safety First

Every one of our coaches is passionate about keeping wrestlers healthy. Wrestling is a contact sport with a lot of bumps and bruises. We have a certified EMT/Sports Medicine Trainer at every match and our coaches must attain the SafeSport certification prior to participating in any club activities.
